---

## 1. Vision
ANTYO System is a Life Operating System designed to help humans optimize time, money, health, relationships, and personal growth using AI and gamification.

## 2. The Problem
Modern life is managed across disconnected apps. This leads to scattered data, low self-awareness, and no real optimization loop. You use one app for your budget, another for your workouts, and another for your calendar. The apps don't talk to each other, so they cannot provide holistic life advice.

## 3. The Solution
ANTYO unifies 5 core life systems into one adaptive platform:
- **Time** (ANTYO Focus)
- **Finance** (ANTYO Finance)
- **Health** (ANTYO Health)
- **Relationships** (ANTYO Connect)
- **Story** (ANTYO Journal/Legacy)

Guided by two AI companions:
- **Azul** (Strategy & Optimization - "The Logic")
- **Azel** (Emotion & Sustainability - "The Heart")

## 5. Data Autonomy & Trust (Core Principle)
ANTYO prioritizes **extreme transparency** and **user control**. ANTYO must always feel like an assistant, not a surveillance system.

- **Per-Domain Control:** Users can choose which life domains the AI may analyze (e.g., enable Time & Finance, disable Relationships).
- **Transparent Data Awareness:** Users can see exactly what data is being collected and how it is used to generate an insight.
- **SABBATH MODE:** A single physical/digital switch that completely pauses:
  - Data collection
  - AI analysis
  - Gamification
  - Automation
  *(Because humans are not machines, and everyone deserves a day completely off the grid).*

## 6. THE GRAND ROADMAP (Multi-Year Strategy)

Membangun ANTYO System tidak bisa dilakukan dalam satu malam. Dibutuhkan pendekatan modular di mana setiap "Pilar" dibangun sebagai aplikasi sukses yang berdiri sendiri sebelum disatukan.

### Phase 1: The Trojan Horse (Time Mastery)
**Produk:** ANTYO Focus
**Status:** In Development (MVP)
**Tujuan:** Memecahkan masalah "Plan vs Reality" untuk waktu. Jika Anda bisa mengontrol waktu seseorang, Anda bisa memprediksi aspek hidup mereka yang lain.
*Fokus saat ini adalah memastikan ANTYO Focus mencapai 100K+ unduhan dan cashflow positif.*

### Phase 2: The Resource Expansion (Money & Health)
**Produk:** ANTYO Finance & ANTYO Health
**Status:** Blueprint (Tahun Ke-2/Ke-3)
**Tujuan:** Membangun aplikasi kedua dan ketiga dengan filosofi UI/UX yang sama (Visual, Gamified, Truth-Tracking).
*Di fase ini, "ANTYO" resmi menjadi sebuah Ekosistem (Brand). Pengguna berlangganan "ANTYO One" untuk mengakses semua modul.*

### Phase 3: The Convergence (The Unified User State)
**Produk:** ANTYO Core (Cloud Data Layer)
**Status:** Long-term
**Tujuan:** Menghubungkan database Time, Finance, dan Health.
*Aplikasi mulai menyadari pola lintas disiplin. Contoh: "Kamu produktif (Time) saat pengeluaran kopimu naik (Finance), tapi kesehatanmu turun (Health) bulan ini."*

### Phase 4: The Companions Awakening (Azul & Azel)
**Produk:** Integration of Large Language Models (LLM) & On-Device ML
**Status:** Long-term
**Tujuan:** Mengaktifkan Azul (AI Strategi Logis) dan Azel (AI Empati/Kesehatan Mental).
*Bukan sekadar *chatbot*, mereka membaca *Unified Data* Anda secara pasif dan memberikan "Nudges" (saran mikro) harian di layar Anda.*

### Phase 5: The Physical Bridge (IoT & Automation)
**Produk:** ANTYO Environment
**Status:** Long-term Vision
**Tujuan:** Menghubungkan *software* dengan dunia nyata (Lampu Pintar, Kunci Pintar, *Wearables*).
*Saat jadwal "Deep Work" di ANTYO Focus dimulai, lampu ruangan otomatis meredup, notifikasi HP mati, dan Azul menugaskan Anda.*
