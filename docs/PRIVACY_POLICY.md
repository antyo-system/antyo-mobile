# Privacy Policy for ANTYO Focus

**Effective Date:** June 5, 2026

ANTYO Focus ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights when you use the ANTYO Focus mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Local-First Data Storage
ANTYO Focus is designed to be a **local-first** application. This means that your core personal data, including your focus sessions, skills, pillars, calendar blocks, and statistics, are stored **locally on your device**. 

We do not have a backend server that collects, stores, or sells your personal focus data. Your data belongs to you.

## 2. Information We Collect
To improve the App and understand how it is used, we collect anonymous usage data using **PostHog** (a privacy-focused analytics tool).

**What is collected anonymously:**
*   **App Events:** Which screens you visit, when a timer is started/stopped, and which features are used.
*   **Device Information:** Operating system version, device model, and general locale settings.
*   **Crash Reports:** Information to help us fix bugs and crashes.

**What is NEVER collected:**
*   The actual names of your skills, pillars, or session titles.
*   Personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
*   Any audio, camera, or biometric data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you in order to provide their service:
*   [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy) (for Android distribution and crashlytics)
*   [PostHog](https://posthog.com/privacy) (for anonymous product analytics)

## 4. Permissions We Request
Currently, ANTYO Focus requires minimal permissions. We do not request Camera, Microphone, or Location permissions in the current version of the App.

## 5. Security
We value your trust in providing us your Data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Since your core data is stored on your device, the security of that data relies heavily on your device's security settings (e.g., screen lock, encryption).

## 6. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
